# CAIOS v4.7 Editorial Checklist and Human Approval

## Scope

This milestone adds checklist persistence, reviewer decisions, immutable decision history, optimistic concurrency checks, row locking, and audit events.

## Safety invariants

- Only authenticated newsroom roles may update checklist fields.
- Only administrator, editor, and reviewer roles may record decisions.
- Approval is accepted only while a story is `awaiting_approval`.
- Approval requires sources, facts, rights, and SEO checks to be complete.
- Actor identity comes from `auth.uid()` and is never accepted from the browser.
- Stale forms are rejected using the story `updated_at` value.
- Approved records become locked to ordinary editing.
- Decisions and audit events are append-only.
- This milestone does not create WordPress drafts, publish, schedule, deploy, or use service-role credentials.

## Staging verification

1. Apply all migrations to a non-production Supabase project.
2. Test checklist updates as researcher, reviewer, and read-only users.
3. Confirm read-only users are denied.
4. Move a test story to `awaiting_approval` through the existing controlled workflow.
5. Confirm approval fails with an incomplete checklist.
6. Complete all four checks and approve as a reviewer.
7. Confirm the story becomes `approved`, `human_approved` becomes true, and both approval and audit rows exist.
8. Submit an old form version and confirm the stale-write rejection.
9. Confirm no publication record or external request is created.

Do not apply to production until these checks are recorded and reviewed.
